    About company

    The concept of Myasnov is based on the idea of good nutrition, healthy lifestyle and revival of cultural traditions. It was this idea that moved us when in 2003 we conceived to open our first store selling fresh meat products. Since then, 17 years have passed, and today MyasnovЪ is the first federal network of healthy food stores, which includes more than 200 stores. Our assortment is constantly increasing - to fresh chilled meat products, sausages and delicacies added natural dairy products and real handicraft bread. Production is carried out at our own factories and at the factories of our partners.

    Whatever the scale of our business, the main thing is always the same for us: we believe that the products we produce should be fresh, delicious, natural, and the seller should always be honest with his buyer. Our business is based on a whole system of principles, which was developed by the company's management at the very beginning of its activity, and then picked up by a growing team of professionals and fixed as corporate standards and values.

    We sincerely believe that:

    Health is the main value in human life, the basis of the future.

    Honesty and respect are the basis of relationships.

    Business must be socially responsible - not in words, but in deeds.

    Any business, let alone one that is related to human well-being, must be done professionally.

    High flying - in a competent combination of modern achievements and the best traditions. It gives a unique opportunity to produce home cooking on an industrial scale.

    And so we:

    We produce only quality products - the one we love ourselves. Our products are natural, give strength, health and energy.
